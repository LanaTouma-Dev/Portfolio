Who I am

Building things that matter

I'm Lana Touma, a full-stack developer and AI engineer. I care about how systems are designed, not just how they're coded. There's something satisfying about a well-drawn architecture, the kind that's obvious in hindsight but took real thinking to get to.

Lately most of my work lives at the edge where software meets intelligence. I build systems that use AI as infrastructure, not decoration. The kind of integrations that quietly make something work better rather than loudly announce themselves.

When I'm not pushing commits, I'm probably redesigning something that didn't need redesigning, deep in a rabbit hole about typography, or brewing a perfect cup of tea.
